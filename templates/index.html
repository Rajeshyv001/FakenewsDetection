Spreading fake news is a punishable offense, So check it before forwarding

When you got a news forwarded by your friends or family members how will you know whether this news is real or just a rumour? This website helps you to detect whether the news is real or fake and provide a warning if the news is fake.These warnings usually encourage people to think critically about any online information which spread rumours or misleading information and think before sharing and avoid clicking on links provided in these misleading news.
